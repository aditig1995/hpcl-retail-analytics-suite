⚙️ Priority Score Engine
▶ 📘 How Priority Score Works

Priority Score Formula

Market Size = 20%
Opportunity Potential = 25%
Gap to Leader = 40%
Growth Trend = 5%
Strategic Position = 10%
Total Score = 100

Priority Bands

Critical  Score ≥ 60
High  Score 45 – 59
Medium  Score 40 – 54
Low  Score < 40

Category Logic

Attack: Leader ≠ HPCL and Gap < 10%
Develop: Leader ≠ HPCL and Gap ≥ 10% with HPCL Share ≥ 20%
Expand: HPCL Share < 20%
Defend: HPCL Leader and Gap to #2 < 5%
Maintain: HPCL Leader and Gap to #2 ≥ 5%

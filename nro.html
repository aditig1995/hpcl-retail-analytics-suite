Scoring Methodology & Data Schema
📐 v6 Road-Based Scoring Model
Final Score = 40% Opportunity + 20% Cannibalization-Adj + 40% Feasibility
All candidates generated ONLY on motorway/trunk/primary/secondary/tertiary roads via OSM Overpass API. Unclassified, residential, service, track, path, and footway roads are hard-rejected. No coordinate offsets — candidates sit exactly on road centerlines. Hard exclusion zones, settlement rule (2 km), and final validation gate ensure zero false white-space.
Market Opportunity Score (0–100)
35% HPCL Coverage Gap · 30% Market Share Deficit · 15% Competitor Volume · 10% Growth Trend · 5% Density · 5% Road Hierarchy
Cannibalization Score (0–100, inverted)
0–2 km from HPCL → 80 pts penalty · 2–5 km → 50 pts · 5–8 km → 25 pts · >8 km → 0 pts
Feasibility Score (0–100) — minimum 50 to qualify
Road hierarchy (0–35) + Outlet proximity (0–30) + Market activity (0–20) + Settlement proximity (0–15)
Road Quality Score (Allowed Roads Only)
National Highway (motorway/trunk): 100
State Highway (primary): 85
Major District Road (secondary): 70
District Road (tertiary): 55
unclassified / residential / service / track / path / footway: REJECTED — not motorable
Hard Exclusion Zones (Mandatory — Never Recommended)
❌ National Parks (Kanger Valley, Indravati, Guru Ghasi Das) — 15 km buffer
❌ Wildlife Sanctuaries (Barnawapara, Achanakmar, Udanti-Sitanadi) — 10 km buffer
❌ Waterfalls (Chitrakote, Tirathgarh) — 8 km buffer
❌ Rivers, Reservoirs, Dams, Water Bodies — 500 m buffer
❌ Dense Forest Zones (Bastar, Dantewada, Bijapur) — 1 km buffer
❌ Off-road locations — only motorway/trunk/primary/secondary/tertiary allowed
❌ No settlement within 2 km — rejected as uninhabited / false white-space
